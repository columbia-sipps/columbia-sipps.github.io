Schedule

SIPPS hosts three parallel workshop series running from the week of June 7 through the week of August 9, each with one session per week. See below for a detailed overview of all workshop topics and dates, and see the workshops page for materials and video recordings associated with each session. Also see the About page to view an introductory video and slide deck about the program.
