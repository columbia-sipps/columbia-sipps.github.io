SIPPS 2023 applications are now open!


Follow this link to apply. Deadline: April 14, 2023



Important Program Details

  • SIPPS 2023 will run from June 12 to August 18.

  • This program intended for students with limited-to-no psychological research experience. There are no coursework or previous research requirements.

  • SIPPS is only open to current Columbia University undergraduate students.
    • This includes all Columbia schools (e.g., Columbia College, General Studies, Barnard College).
    • This does not include students who are graduating in spring 2023.

  • SIPPS 2023 will take place virtually.

  • Unfortunately, we cannot offer funding to SIPPS mentees at this time.


Questions or concerns?

While the application period is open, we will be holding weekly, informal info sessions (via zoom) where you can drop in and ask any questions you have about SIPPS (e.g., the application process, program content, other personal concerns, etc.).

These info sessions will take place from 3-4pm on Fridays throughout the application period (3/17, 3/24, 3/31, 4/7, & 4/15) at this zoom link.
