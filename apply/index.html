Applications for SIPPS 2022 are now open!

Apply now at this link.


SIPPS 2022 will run from the week of June 6 through August 12.

Please note that this program is specifically designed for students with little to no prior research experience, and for students from marginalized and underrepresented communities. There are no coursework, minimum GPA, or prior experience requirements.

At this time, SIPPS is not able to offer funding for trainees. However, we highly encourage you to check out this spreadsheet for a list of summer funding resources.

The deadline for all applications is April 1st, 2022 at 11:59pm.



Have any questions about the application process?

  • Beginning on March 4, we will be hosting open Q&A sessions on Fridays from 10-11am (ET) via Zoom at this link. Feel free to stop by with any questions or concerns, no matter how small!
  • You can also reach out sippscupsych@gmail.com.
