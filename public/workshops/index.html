Workshops

As part of the SIPPS program, you will participate in three different workshop series intended to provide you with the conceptual background & hands-on research skills needed to formulate, execute, and describe your projects.

Visit the links below to access recordings of each workshop session (posted shortly after the original workshop date), as well as any files or materials referenced as part of them.

Coding (basic)

Learn how to use R to clean, analyze, and visualize your data.

Coding (advanced)

Data analysis workshops for those more familiar with coding principles and/or the R language.

Professional Development / Journal Club

Learn about academic research and related scientific careers, as well as how to digest and think critically about contemporary journal articles.

Research Skills

Acquire valuable skills for conducting psychological research, from conducting literature reviews to developing your research question(s) to sharing your results with peers.
